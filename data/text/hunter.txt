First flew on 28th September 1956 and after a spell at 5 Maintenance Unit it was assigned to 66(F) Squadron at RAF Acklington on 29th October 1956. After 66 Squadron’s disbandment, the aircraft moved to 92(F) Squadron in County Durham. 92 Squadron formed the RAF’s aerobatic display team named ‘The Blue Diamonds’ and XG226 joined the team. The aircraft also served with 229 Operational Conversion Unit and Nos.1 and.2 Tactical Weapon Units. The cockpit section of this aircraft arrived at the museum on 23rd January, 2003.
